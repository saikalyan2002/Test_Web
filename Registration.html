WISE is the premier academic research forum for the discussion of information systems issues through the lens of economics. We expect researchers at WISE 2020 to bring forth cutting-edge research that studies how IT, in all its forms, addresses important business and societal questions. Modern IT-enabled phenomena have wide ranging impacts for consumers, firms, industries, and markets, making this a fascinating time to be an economics of IS researcher.
